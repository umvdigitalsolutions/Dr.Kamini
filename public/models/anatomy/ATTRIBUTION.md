# Anatomy model attribution

## Muscle model

The `muscular.glb` geometry is adapted and optimized from the Optima muscle
model published by Adamas Advanced Designs. The Optima model is derived from
BodyParts3D data and uses Z-Anatomy naming references.

- Source: https://www.adamasdesigns.com/licenses
- BodyParts3D: © Database Center for Life Science (DBCLS), licensed by NBDC
- License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International
- License URL: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/
- Changes: geometry simplification and Meshopt compression for web delivery

## Skeleton model

`skeleton.glb` is adapted from “Open3DModel - Skeleton - English labels” by
the Open3D project, George J.R. Maat (LUMC), Eungyeol Lee (LUMC), and
contributors.

- Source: https://anatomytool.org/content/open3dmodel-skeleton-english-labels
- License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International
- License URL: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/
- Changes: geometry simplification, texture resizing, WebP conversion,
  Meshopt compression, and mirrored appendicular structures for web delivery
